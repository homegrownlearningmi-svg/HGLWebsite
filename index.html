Meet Angel

Teaching with warmth, wonder, and real experience.

Angel Floyd has spent 30 years working in early childhood development and helping children discover the world around them. Her favorite lessons are the ones children can experience firsthand—holding a chick, observing an animal's behavior, exploring a plant, or asking the unexpected question that opens a new line of learning.

Through Homegrown Learning, Angel brings farm animals and practical teaching directly to schools, churches, and community groups. Her Christian faith is integral to how she teaches children about nature, stewardship, and the created world.

Common questions

Useful details before you inquire.

Which animals can Angel bring?

The exact animals should be confirmed for each event based on availability, travel conditions, the venue, and the children's ages. The final website should list specific animals only after Angel confirms what she can reliably offer.

How much does an event cost?

Events are quoted individually. Price can depend on travel distance, event length, number of sessions, group size, animals, and any special program requirements.

Is the Christian perspective part of every program?

Yes. Angel teaches from a Christian understanding of creation, stewardship, and the value of children. The lesson is still planned appropriately for the children's ages and the event setting, but her faith is not treated as an optional add-on.

How far does Angel travel?

The normal service area is approximately 45 minutes from Grant, Michigan. Requests outside that area can still be discussed, but travel may affect availability and pricing.

Can families join the homeschool group?

Families can join the interest list for community activities. Angel can then send invitations as suitable nature, farm, or practical-learning events are planned.
